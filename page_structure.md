## CRITICAL IMPLEMENTATION PRINCIPLES

1. **No Administrative Language**  
   - ❌ "Records", "Championships", "Statistics"  
   - ✅ "Hall of Fame", "Championship Battles", "Career Arc"  

2. **Normalized Collections Power Every Page**  
   - `Race Weekend` = Events (context) + Sessions (structure) + Highlights (moments) + Galleries (media) + Turnings (narrative pivot)  
   - `Driver Profile` = Drivers (core) + Journeys (path) + Aspirations (voice) + Skills (competence) + Footages (proof)  

3. **Zero Siloed Data Pages**  
   - Stats live *within context*: "See [Driver]'s fastest laps" on Driver Profile  
   - No standalone "Results" or "Standings" pages — embedded in narrative flows  

4. **Ecommerce as Story Extension**  
   - Every product links to narrative: "This cap celebrates [Driver]'s Monaco victory — watch the highlight"  
   - Products inherit context from Drivers/Events/Cars via normalized relations  

5. **Legal Minimalism**  
   - Policies exist *only* in footer — never in primary navigation  
   - Trust signals woven into brand experience (e.g., "Secure Checkout" badges in Shop)  

---

This structure transforms your normalized architecture into a cohesive fan experience. Every page consumes multiple abstract collections to assemble emotional narratives — never exposing raw data. The backend complexity serves frontend simplicity. No ERP traces. Pure brand storytelling.
