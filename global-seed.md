## IDENTITY

**Vision Statement**
We race to prove what is possible — on track, in the workshop, and in everything we build.

**Mission Statement**
AF Motorsport competes at the highest level of Porsche racing while building a team, a culture, and a body of work that outlasts any single result. We develop drivers, engineer remarkable machines, and document everything with the honesty and depth it deserves.

**Founding Story**
*(Rich Text)*
AF Motorsport was born from a simple obsession: that racing is not just about speed, but about the relentless pursuit of perfection. Founded by a group of engineers and drivers who felt disconnected from the sterile nature of modern motorsport, we set out to build a team that values craft, culture, and storytelling as much as podiums. From our first garage in Stuttgart to the international stages of the Porsche Championship, our story is one of grit, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to the human element of racing.
